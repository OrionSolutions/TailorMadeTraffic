Advertising Campaign

Collaborate with our artists in creating one captivating ad that could really deliver. We'll take care of the whole process--from the conception to the monitoring of this campaign. Our experienced marketers will walk you through every step.

Conversion Tracking

As part of our commitment to monitor your ads after launch, we'll help you set up AdWords conversion tracking--this way, you'll be able to see for yourself how productive your campaigns are. We'll show you customer's activity, including newsletter signups, website purchases, phone calls, app downloads, and more.

 
 
Lead Tracking

Using our smart tracking metrics, we'll help you follow the origin of your leads in real-time and learn how your ads have reached them. This makes it easier for you to attribute your leads to your next campaigns. Our dynamic lead tracking feature can accurately give you a glimpse of which specific demographic responds to your ad campaigns.

Support: Ticketing System

Accelerate your customer response process through our ticket assignment system that can adapt to your line of business. We can create a workflow automation that can delegate certain processes to certain staff, and escalate concerns when needed.

 
 
Professional Marketing Consultation

You can collaborate with our talented artists, web experts, and strategists to plot your next steps in your campaign. We do this by carefully analysing the comprehensive data produced by our tracking tools, and those that appear in your dashboard.
